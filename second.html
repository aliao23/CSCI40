THINGS I LIKE ABOUT CMC

THE ARCHITECTURE

Claremont McKenna is located on a "a scenic, tree-lined residential campus 30 miles east of Los Angeles": a beautiful campus with modern features such as the Roberts Pavilion and the Kravis Centre. The Cube, also known as the Living Room, is pictured above.

THE TEACHING STYLE

As Claremont McKenna is an American liberal arts college, it has a distinctive teaching style in comparison to larger research universities.

  1. Small class sizes
  2. Dedicated professors
  3. Inter-5C resource pool

THE PEOPLE

CMC has a very close-knit community with a diverse range of interests and values, meaning that every year when student government is run there's a huge variety of candidates. The list of clubs and institutes can be seen here!
