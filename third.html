Claremont McKenna's Clubs and Institutes

Most clubs at CMC are affiliated with ASCMC, which stands for Associated Students of Claremont McKenna. The organisation is democratic and run by an elected Executive Board, which determines funding towards lots of societies as well as creating events.

Most clubs at CMC can be grouped into three main sections:

Academic clubs

These are usually associated with one of the subjects or majors offered at CMC.

  • American Medical Student Association
  • Chinese Political and Societal Studies Club
  • Claremont Accounting Association
  • Claremont Consulting Group
  • Claremont International Relations Society
  • ... and many more!

Identity clubs

CMC has a diverse community that aims to highlight and promote each other's differences!

  • 1 Gen
  • Asian Pacific American Mentors
  • Black Student Association
  • CMS Christian Athletes
  • Claremont QuestBridge Scholars Network
  • ... and many more!

Music and arts clubs

Despite the stereotype that CMC is mainly pre-business and all work, we have a thriving musician and artist community present.

  • 5C Records
  • Claremont Creative Coterie
  • CREATE Club
  • Fashion Photography and Design
  • Film Appreciation and Learning Club
  • ... and many more!
